##### Biohazard (Full Package starting at $600)
(Gold+Ozone)
- In the event that a vehicle has been exposed to a biohazard, such as bodily fluids, vomit, or other hazardous materials, our biohazard cleaning service is designed to thoroughly clean and sanitize the affected areas. This service includes the use of specialized cleaning agents and equipment to ensure that all traces of the biohazard are removed, leaving the vehicle safe and odor-free.
- **If we determine a biohazard is present, we will provide a detailed assessment and requote for the necessary cleaning services.**
  - (The best value for this service is to upgrade your package to at least Gold and the biohazard service includes the Ozone treatment for bacterial odor removal.)
- Due to health and hazard concerns, we require a consultation to assess the extent of the biohazard contamination and determine the appropriate cleaning methods. Pricing for this service will vary based on the severity of the contamination and the size of the vehicle.
FAQ: What contaminants do you handle, and which do you not?
 - We handle a wide range of biohazards, including bodily fluids, vomit, and other hazardous materials from humans and animals. 
 - We do not handle:
   - Blood, disease, body parts, drug related materials, or any other biohazard that may pose a significant health risk to our staff or customers.
