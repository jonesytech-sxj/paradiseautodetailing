Getting an accurate quote

The more detail you give us upfront, the more accurate your first quote will be. Here is what helps.

What to include when you reach out

  • Your name and best phone number or email
  • Vehicle year, make, and model
  • Vehicle type: sedan, SUV, truck, van, or other
  • The package or service you are interested in
  • Interior condition: pet hair, stains, odors, or heavy buildup
  • Exterior condition: road grime, tar, sap, or water spots
  • Your location and preferred appointment timing
  • Photos if you have them — they help us prepare

Quick copy-and-send template

Hi Paradise Auto Detailing, I need a quote for my [year / make / model] in [city or area]. I am interested in [service or package]. The vehicle has [pet hair / stains / odor / heavy grime]. My preferred timing is [day or timeframe].
